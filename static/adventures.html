What is TEDxAdventure?

Adventures are unique and intimate events designed to offer participants unparalleled, behind-the-scenes access to the people and places making the local community so dynamic. The goal of every adventure is for participants to meet and interact with the masterminds behind the future of technology, business, and entertainment (to name a few). As many of our adventures are cross-generational by design, and open to all, we can stimulate conversations among people of all ages, professions and neighborhoods.
